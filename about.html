About us

WHO WE ARE

Ruiru Mount Hermon Academy is a private primary school admitting children for Play Group, PP1 and PP2. We are located on 1st Sunrise, Avenue Kihunguro, Opposite Gichoya Water Development Company. Our classroom are spacious together with a large compound that is condusive for young children to learn and exploit their talents. In addition, our playing facilities include swings and slides for children to play and enjoy. We have transport facilities for children from distant places.

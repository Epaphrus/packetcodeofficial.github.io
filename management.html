The Founders

Rev. Joseph Murage

Rev. Joseph Murage, is a graduate from University of Nairobi with BSc in Statistics and Masters in Population Studies from the same University. He has over 15 years of experience in Monitoring & Evaluation and Project implementation. Currently he is working as Monitoring & Evaluation Specialist with an inter-governmental organization providing geospatial services in the Eastern and Southern Africa Region. Together with his wife, Pastor Eudias, they are Founders and Directors of the School. In addition, they serve the Lord as Ministers in Kenya Redeemed Church, Ruiru where the school is also located.

Pastor Eudias Muthii

Pastor Eudias Muthii is as a Primary School teacher with over 30 years teaching experience in in both rural and urban schools with a passion to influence positively young children with education. She brings a wealth of experience as a result of her exemplary service in public schools. She is instrumental in guiding our teachers to provide model quality education with value for money.

SCHOOL ADMINSTRATOR

Moses Muthii

Moses Muthii, is a Certified Public Accountant, CPA (K) and graduate from KCA University with Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting Option). He has a wealth of experience handling and performing, accounting, finance and auditing, accounts payables and receivables, compliance, analysis, tax and statutory.
